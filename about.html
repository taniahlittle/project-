More About Highly Anticipated

Highly Anticipated Was Created On November 27,2015. It Was Started By Jacquana Foxworth And Funded By The Head Of The Laffyette Garden Community Center Which Is Monique Smalls. Ms.Foxworth Was Inspired By Going To A Competitive Competition In Her Past And Decide To Create Group Of Her Own, She Started With 8 Dancers And Both Genders From Ages 6-21 And Won 2nd Place For Their First Show In 2015. The Original Colors That They Started With Was Yellow/Gold And Black Which Is Also Their Signature Color Along With Crowns.
